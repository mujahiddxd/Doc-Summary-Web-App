How Our Summarizer Works

Extractive Summarization

Our extractive summarization identifies and extracts key sentences from your original document to create a concise summary. This approach preserves the original wording while reducing content to essential information only.

  • Maintains original language and context
  • Extracts the most informative sentences
  • Perfect for factual documents and reports

Abstractive Summarization

Our abstractive summarization uses AI to understand content and generate new sentences that capture the essence of your document. This creates more natural, cohesive summaries with rephrased content.

  • Creates new, concise phrasing
  • Produces more readable, flowing summaries
  • Ideal for articles, papers, and creative content

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between extractive and abstractive summarization?

Extractive summarization pulls out existing sentences from your text, while abstractive summarization generates new sentences that capture the meaning of your content. Extractive maintains original phrasing while abstractive creates entirely new text.

How long does the summarization process take?

Most documents are summarized within seconds, though larger files may take slightly longer to process.

How accurate are the summaries?

Our AI models have been trained on millions of documents to ensure high-quality summaries that capture the essential information of your text. While no automated system is perfect, our algorithms consistently produce summaries that maintain the key points and context of the original content.

What file formats are supported?

Currently, we support plain text (.txt) files for direct upload. You can also paste text directly from any source into our text input field regardless of the original format.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we take data privacy very seriously. Your documents are processed securely, and we do not store the content of your documents beyond the time needed for processing.
